What happens when someone is suppressed?

  • The email is blocked from ALL future bulk sends
  • The lead gets a unsubscribed badge
  • Individual sends to that lead are also blocked with a warning
  • The suppression is permanent unless you manually remove it
⚠️ Note: This does NOT improve spam placement. Spam filters check SPF/DKIM/DMARC, sender reputation, and engagement — not unsubscribe links. This feature is about compliance and respecting recipient choice.
